About Us


The Korean Undergraduate Student Association of the Vanderbilt University consists of students whose goal is to promote awareness and appreciation of traditional and modern aspects of rich Korean culture in and outside the Vanderbilt community. The orgnanization and its various events are open to anyone who are interested in the Korean culture and its community.

Our activities and events include:
• General Body Meetings with FREE FOOD!
• Family groups
• “Bae” System (Hoobae/Sunbae System)
• Culture Show collaboration with other International organizations on campus
• Social Events including Pepero Fundraising, Kimbap Making
• Opportunities to interact with other cultural groups on campus
• Unforgettable memories throughout your college career
