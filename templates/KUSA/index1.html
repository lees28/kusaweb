Welcome to Vanderbilt
Korean Undergraduate Student Association


Vanderbilt University's Korean Students Association, a non-profit student-run organization, promotes cultural and social events on and off campus to celebrate its rich Korean heritage. Through events, programs, and other club functions, we strive to achieve a unified and inclusive community on campus. KUSA strives to promote awareness of Korean culture by collaborating with our Korean affiliated performance organizations. We also participate in university-sponsored events throughout the year that promote multicultural awareness and celebrate the diversity of the Vanderbilt community.
