Freshman Welcome Party!

April 11, 2015, 11 AM at Keohane Atrium

The freshmen welcome party for incoming Vandy students in Korea is almost here! It's a chance for Sunbae's of Vandy's Korean community to meet their Class of 2020 Hoobae's and vice versa. All Vanderbilt students in Korea, current and incoming, are invited for some good Samgyupsal and a social night of turnin up.
